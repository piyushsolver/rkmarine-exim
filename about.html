About RK Marine Exim

Delivering Quality Marine Parts Worldwide Since 2020

Our Maritime Legacy

We, RK Marine Exim, India are one of the leading Ship machinery parts Export companies with experience in the marine industry since 2020. At RK Marine Exim, we specialize in supplying high-quality engine parts and other machinery equipment which we procure from demolished vessels at Alang ship recycling yard, India.

We provide all kind of ship machinery parts in New/Unused, Used & Reconditioned condition.

Global Reach

We are already serving clients worldwide on the land and the ocean. Our Client base Includes, Ship management companies, Ship repair companies, Power and energy generation plants and our fellow traders across the globe.

By providing Quality parts and competitive rates, Our Company Founder Mr. Harsh Rajyaguru keep maintaining our clients satisfied and Build good partnerships with them.

Our Core Offerings

  • 2 & 4 Stroke engine parts
  • Complete unit of Auxiliary engines
  • Air and refrigeration compressors
  • Automation and Navigation parts
  • Marine pumps with motors

Quality Assurance

We provide all parts only after inspecting and performing Quality tests. We offer machinery parts in New/Unused, Reconditioned and Used in good condition. We offer Genuine and OEM parts.
